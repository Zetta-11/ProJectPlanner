Today's work environment is full of challenges and opportunities that demand from teams and individual users not only high productivity but also effective tools for managing tasks and projects. The lack of convenient tools can become an obstacle on the way to achieving the set goals, causing a loss of time, ineffective planning, and unsuccessful control over processes. In this context, there is an urgent need to create an integrated task management system that not only meets the requirements of the time but also becomes an integral part of the successful functioning of business structures.
